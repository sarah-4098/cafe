This family-run business was established in 1976 and is located in one of the hidden corners of Central London. This vintage style cafe serves coffees and breakfasts to suit whatever your taste. Fresh hand-made coffee beans are used to make your coffee so that it is the best quality possible. We hope you enjoy your stay here!
